About Us

A Proud Division of FelipeTadd

FT Fashion, a branch of FelipeTadd, is a leading clothing manufacturer specializing in producing and delivering high-quality apparel and home textiles. With a commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability, we cater to global fashion brands and retailers, ensuring premium craftsmanship and on-time delivery. Backed by the expertise and legacy of FelipeTadd, FT Fashion upholds the highest industry standards, bringing style and comfort to every collection we create.

At FT Fashion, we specialize in delivering premium-quality uniforms and T-shirts tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses, organizations, and individuals. With years of experience in the industry, we take pride in offering a seamless blend of quality, style, and affordability.

Our collection is carefully designed to combine functionality, comfort, and style, ensuring that every product meets the needs of modern professionals. Whether you're looking for sleek corporate uniforms or versatile plain T-shirts for events or branding, we have you covered.
